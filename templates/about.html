Our Team

We started as a group of friends, sharing similar interests. On long summer nights, we often found ourselves with a drink in hand talking about music and culture until the early mornings. These constant conversations led us to first investigate starting a podcast. Availability difficulties put paid to that idea but then someone threw out the idea of a website, a blog to be specific. One were each of us could post the latest things we all talk about in our own free time.

This is where the concept of this blog was born. The name stemming from the transparent process of us sending ideas, tidbits and news on to each other through the blog. It also comes from a group favourite song by D'angelo, Send It On. We wanted to make this site with content coming from topics we care about individually and as a group. Music lovers and culture keepers from around the world. We all do different things and have different experiences in life but we all share the love for the content we bring to this blog so we hope you enjoy! If anyone has questions, ideas or would like to work with us as a group, feel free to get at us on our contact page.
